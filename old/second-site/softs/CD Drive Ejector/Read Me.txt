CD Drive Ejector 2.0
--------------------

INSTALLATION
------------
* Unzip CDEjectorI.zip.
* Copy CDEjectorI.exe and paste it into a directory. Eg: C:\CDDE
* Run CDEjectorI.exe and follow the instruction on the screen.

* After installing press Alt+Ctrl+ <CDDrive Letter>.
  Drive tray atomatically Open/Close.
